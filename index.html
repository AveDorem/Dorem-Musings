Hi, I'm Dorem, and welcome to my corner of the web! I spend most of my time reading, writing, and trying to make sense of things; through language, code, and thought. I like systems that truly mean something, not some noise that fills space.

I study the old world: Latin, Greek, history, faith. It reminds me that we're not as modern as we think. I still believe in structure, discipline, and truth, even when I fail at them.

I listen to music that sounds alive: alt-rock, black/death-metal, punk, breakcore, shoegaze, and various other genres. I train to stay focused, and I write to stay consistent.

This site isn't meant to impress anyone. It's just a trace of what I've built, what I've read, and what I've tried to understand.
